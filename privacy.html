Privacy Policy

Last updated: Wed, Jul 9, 2025

Information We Collect

Sip & Scroll collects minimal information necessary for app functionality:

  • Photos taken during hydration verification (temporarily processed for cup detection)
  • App usage patterns for blocking functionality
  • Hydration tracking data (stored locally)
  • Personal moments and reflection data (stored locally)

Photo Processing

When you take a hydration selfie:

  • Photos are temporarily sent to Google's Gemini AI for cup/water verification
  • Only the verification result (cup detected: yes/no) is returned to the app
  • Photos are not stored by Gemini or our servers

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for:

  • Verifying hydration actions through AI image analysis
  • Providing hydration tracking functionality
  • Enabling app blocking features
  • Creating personal reflection moments

Data Storage

Your personal data is handled as follows:

  • Hydration logs and personal moments are stored locally on your device
  • Photos are temporarily processed by Google Gemini AI for verification only
  • No photos or personal information are permanently stored on external servers

Third-Party Services

We use Google Gemini AI for photo verification. This service:

  • Receives photos temporarily for cup detection analysis
  • Returns only verification results (not photos)
  • Is governed by Google's privacy policy
  • Does not store or retain your photos

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our support form.
