Common Questions

How does the app blocking work?

Sip & Scroll uses iOS Screen Time API to temporarily block access to your chosen apps until you complete a hydration action.

What happens to my photos?

Photos are temporarily sent to Google's Gemini AI to verify you have a cup/water. Only the verification result is returned - photos are not stored by us or Google.

Can I use "I'm All Set" without drinking?

Yes! The "I'm All Set" button lets you defer to mindful scrolling when you're genuinely hydrated. This choice is tracked as a positive mindful moment.

How does Premium work?

Premium unlocks unlimited app blocking, app categories, website blocking, and Personal Moments - your weekly reflective journey.
